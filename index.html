❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a habit?

Tap the "+" button on the Today screen and follow the setup wizard to create your habit.

Can I use Habito without an account?

Yes! You can start without an account. Tap "Start without an account" on the welcome screen.

How do reminders work?

Go to Settings → Notifications to enable smart reminders for your habits.

Is my data private and secure?

Your data is stored locally on your device. Optional cloud sync uses end-to-end encrypted HTTPS. We never sell your data.

How do I delete a habit?

Open the habit detail page, tap the three-dot menu in the top right, and select "Delete Habit".

🔒 Privacy Policy

Habito collects minimal data necessary for app functionality. We do not share or sell your personal data to third parties. All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Settings screen.
