❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What does Habito do?

Habito is an iOS habit tracker that helps you build and maintain daily habits. Track streaks, set reminders, and visualize your progress over time.

How do I create a habit?

Tap the "+" button on the Today screen and follow the setup wizard. You can choose frequency, set reminders, and pick an icon.

Can I use Habito without an account?

Yes! Tap "Start without an account" on the welcome screen. Your data stays on-device. You can sign in later to sync across devices.

How do notifications work?

Go to Settings → Notifications to enable reminders. Each habit can have its own reminder time.

How do I delete my account and data?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

How do I delete a habit?

Open the habit detail page, tap the menu (···) in the top right corner, and select "Delete Habit".
