## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I create a habit?**
Tap the "+" button on the Today screen and follow the setup wizard.

**Can I use Habito without an account?**
Yes! You can start without an account and optionally sign in later to sync across devices.

**How do reminders work?**
Go to Settings → Notifications to enable reminders for your habits.

**Is my data private?**
Yes. Your data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced with your account.

## Privacy Policy
